Disc Golf is a great way to enjoy the outdoors. Grand Forks has multiple courses that can be enjoyed by everyone. Some of these courses are inside city parks as well (Lincoln Park and Riverside Park). The whole family can come and enjoy the many designated spots for gatherings, playgrounds, open areas for misc. activities, restrooms, etc.

Remember to always be curtious to other players and park visitors. Everyone enjoying the outdoors is there to have fun. Remember to be kind to others that are also there to enjoy their time. Plenty of trash containers have been placed all throughout these parks. Please no littering. Please try to leave the courses in better shape than when you got there.

NOTE: Manvel is a few miles North of Grand Forks but worth the drive!
