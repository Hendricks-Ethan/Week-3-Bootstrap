Food & Drink

If your looking for the best burger in town, JL Beers is the place to go. They also have the best selection of craft brews with their 40 tap beers.

JL Beers

Swing on over to The Hub if your feeling more like pud snacks. Sloppy joe's, wraps, tacos, you name it. This palce has a little bit of everything, and you sure get a bang for you buck. They also have a really great patio.

The Hub Pub

If you are an avid sushi lover then this is the place to be. Sakura has such a giant selection of authentic Japanese dishes, the only bad part is having to choose between all the great options.

Sakura

If the craving for the day is some authentic Mexican food, then this is only way to go. Charras and Tequila never disappoints when you need a little bit of heat and flavor in your life.

Charras and Tequila

Now if flavor is what your craving then look no further. House of Punjab is hands down some of the very best authentic Indian food you can find around this area. Very affordable too.

House of Punjab

The pizza here is to die for. Boss' Pizza and Chicken literally tells you what they are in their name. They are bosses at pizza and chicken, and they will never disappoint.

Boss' Pizza & Chicken

Night Life

Are you the type of person that loves to be one with the crowd? Then Joe Black's is the place for you. This is where everyone tries to get to by the end of the night. A great oppurtunity to meet a lot of new people.

Joe Black's

Needing to get out on the dnace floor and just start moving? Level 10 is just the night club for that. It is only open on the weekends, making the availability sparce throughout the week. However, if your willing to be parient for the weekend, it is worth the wait.

Level 10

If live entertainment is what you crave then your in luck. There is live entertainment nearly every weekend at the Southtown Pourhouse. This place is always a good time for those who appreciate live performances.

Southtown Pourhouse

Wanting a more relaxed and chill vibe for the night? Come to O'Reallys and hang out. This is one of the better places to unwind in a stress-free environment, while being away from the more crowded places.

O'Really's Irish Pub
